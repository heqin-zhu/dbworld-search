Call for Postdoctoral Researcher in Big Data Management and Analysis

? Job Description:
We are looking for a highly motivated postdoctoral researcher to conduct efficient management, processing, and analysis of big spatiotemporal data.
Part of the work consists in:
 - Scalable machine learning and/or stream analytics
 - Distributed data management and parallel processing
 - Data modeling and querying for 3D geospatial information systems: BIM, CityGML
 - Lead the development of open-source software

? Expected start date: ASAP (starting on January 2019)
? Duration: Two years with possibility of extension up to 5 years

? Requirements:
 - Applicants must have a PhD degree conferred within the last 7 years at the time of employment.
 - Applicants should be interested by research activities in general and be able to interact with different actors from different domains.
 - Applicants should not have work experience in AIST as an AIST Postdoctoral Researcher for more than 5 years in total.
 - Applicants must be able to communicate in English or Japanese.
 - Strong analytical skills 
 - Good mathematical skills
 - Good coding skills

? Working conditions:
 - Full-time (5 days a week 7 hours and 45 minutes a day, Flextime system available).
 - Paid leave system, commuting allowance system, full social insurance.
 - Working at AIST Tokyo Waterfront (https://www.airc.aist.go.jp/en/access/).

? Application:
 - Mailing or Email; please indicate title as "Application documents for Postdoc".
 - CV with a list of publications, certificate of Ph.D, and names and contact addresses (email addresses) of two references. 
 - Submitted documents will not be returned to the applicant, and managed safely according to strict rules. Personal information received during the recruiting process will be used only for recruitment screening and appointment procedures, and not for any other purposes.

? Screening process: Documents and (video) interview.
