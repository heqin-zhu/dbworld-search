Call For Application to HKBU - Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS)

=====================================================

The Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS) is calling for application. This is a prestigious yet competitive scholarship which aims to attract the best and brightest students from all over the world to pursue their PhD studies in Hong Kong.  Successful applicants will be provided with the following package by the Hong Kong Research Grants Council (RGC) and Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU):

HKPFS Applicants				Scholarship  
Nominated by HKBU and awarded by RG		Approx. HKD 1,640,000 - 1,820,000
Nominated by HKBU but not awarded by RGC	Approx. HKD 920,000 ?C 960,000


Visit http://www.comp.hkbu.edu.hk/hkpfs for more detailed information.

Application Process
*******************
An initial application should be made through the Online Application System at the RGC??s website. After obtaining the official reference number from the RGC Online Application System, a full application should then be submitted to the Graduate School, HKBU through the online application system. 
Interested students are encouraged to contact potential faculty members to discuss research plans and topics AS EARLY AS POSSIBLE. 


Application Deadline
********************
HKBU-CS internal deadline  : 5 October 2018 (first batch); 5 November 2018 (second batch) 
Initial application to RGC : 1 December 2018 at Hong Kong Time 12:00 noon (GMT +8 hours)
Full application to HKBU   : 1 December 2018 at Hong Kong Time 23:59pm (GMT +8 hours)

*The result of the HKPFS 2019/20 will be announced by late March/April 2019.
