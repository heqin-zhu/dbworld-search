The Computer Science department at Umm Al-Qura University invites applications for multiple faculty positions in all ranks.  Applications are welcome from PhD holders in any area of Computer Science. Preference will be given to applicants with expertise in Machine Learning, Distributed Systems, Software Engineering, and Cybersecurity.


The Computer Science department at UQU is a well-established department located at the College of Computer and Information Systems, UQU in Makkah, Saudi Arabia. The department offers undergraduate and master programs. Applicants are expected to teach undergraduate and graduate courses, actively involve in research, as well as supervise master students and contribute to the curriculum development and managerial responsibilities in the department. 

The review is immediate and positions will remain open until filled. Please note that the final hiring is pending budget approval.

Applicants must submit a cover letter, curriculum vitae, and the names of three references to cis_cs@uqu.edu.sa
