POSTDOC POSITIONS IN CARNEGIE MELLON UNIVERSITY - SILICON VALLEY, USA
EMPIRICAL SOFTWARE ENGINEERING

One or two PostDoc/researcher positions in empirical software engineering are available in the Service Oriented Computing Lab (http://hawking.sv.cmu.edu/) at Carnegie Mellon University - Silicon Valley, under Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. The appointment shall start in January 2019 or after and has a duration of 1 or 2 years with the possibility of a 1 year extension.

The research is embedded in several government-funded projects. Specific areas of research and development interests for the position include, but not limited to:
- Data mining and machine learning techniques, especially deep learning, reinforcement learning, graph mining (Graph CNN), tensor methods;
- Natural language processing and knowledge graph techniques;
- Workflow/process mining and recommender system;
- Large-scale software system development in the Cloud

Main responsibilities of the PostDoc position:
- perform scientific research for the projects,
- develop (prototyping) systems and conduct experiments,
- help coordinate Ph.D. students and master students,
- publish in prestigious international conferences and journals

We seek highly motivated and ambitious candidates with a deep interest to work on algorithmic aspects of data analysis with strong programming skills. An ideal candidate has a PhD in computer science or a related field, with research experience in software engineering or related fields with publications in top outlets. Programming with Java and Python is required.

Interested applicants should submit a letter of interest highlighting their research interests and a CV to Prof. Jia Zhang (jia.zhang@sv.cmu.edu).

We will start to review applications until the positions are filled.
