Asian Journal of Computer and Information Systems (AJCIS) is a forum for scientists, researchers, academics, engineers, and practitioners in all aspects of computer science and information systems disciplines publishing high quality and refereed research papers. The journal covers the leading edge subjects and matters in the computer science and information systems in academia, research, business and industry.  The Journal stresses on academic excellence, research rigidity, knowledge distribution, and reciprocated scholarly efforts in order to endorse theoretical, experimental and practical research at national and international levels.
