The Department of Computer Science at the University of New Mexico (UNM) invites applications for an Assistant Professor position. We seek applicants demonstrating excellence in any area of Computer Science or related disciplines.

UNM CS is a collegial department of 17 tenure-track faculty housed in the new Farris Engineering Center. Our research and instruction push the boundaries at the intersection of Computer Science and other fields as well as core Computer Science. UNM is the premier research university in New Mexico, a Carnegie R1 Research Institution and a federally designated Hispanic Serving Institution. Our urban campus is in Albuquerque, an ethnically diverse city with a rich culture, fantastic weather, and unparalleled opportunities for outdoor adventure. 

Applications must include a cover letter, curriculum vitae, research statement, teaching statement, and four letters of reference. Applicants may also include in their application an optional diversity statement. For more information, visit UNM CS.
