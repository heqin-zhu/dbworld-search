CHIN 2019 solicits research paper submissions from academia, industry and government, addressing Health Informatics, Analytics and related areas. 

Health Informatics is a multidisciplinary area which is of key interest as we look on providing affordable medical care to the citizens of a nation. Hence in this workshop we want to explore the challenges in deploying Health Informatics Solutions. Papers are invited for the workshop and topics of interest include but not limited to .
		Cloud Based Health Care Systems-Architecture Models
		Health Informatics Analytics, knowledge discovery, and predictive modeling
		Architectural models for Health Informatic Systems
		Human factors, Interface design, and user studies of health informatics applications.
		Security and Privacy of Health Care data
		m Health, e-health and Telemedicine
		Internet of Things in Health care
		Effective public health monitoring and handling non communicable disease
		Health Data provenance
		Multi View Machine Learning Models 
		Collaborative Models for Mining Health Informatics Data in Distributed environment 

PAPER SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

This workshop is soliciting submissions as either regular or short (concise contribution) papers. At least one author of each accepted paper must register for the conference and present the paper.

Regular Papers should not exceed 10 pages double column including figures, tables, and references in standard ACM format. Additional details may be included in a clearly-marked appendix (containing all technical ideas and proofs necessary for an expert to fully verify the claims of the paper) that will be read at the discretion of the program committee.

Papers must be submitted electronically in printable PDF form via the https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=chin2019
