****** SIGMOD Industrial Track Call For Papers ****** 

http://sigmod2019.org/industrycfp

The Industrial Track of SIGMOD 2019 covers innovative commercial data management systems, solutions, and architectures, database implementations, novel applications of database technology, and experience in applying recent research advances to real-world problems. 
We invite submissions in any of these areas, with the requirement that at least one co-author has a non-academic affiliation. 
Authors are encouraged to contact the track chairs if they need clarification regarding the fit of their work to the track. 

Submission Guidelines 
We accept full papers (up to 12 pages + 4 pages for references and an appendix) and extended abstracts (2-4 pages + 2 pages for references). We encourage submissions on innovative solutions and applications related to commercial or industrial-strength software. At least one of the authors needs to have a non-academic affiliation. 

The papers should be prepared using the latest ACM paper format (2017). The font size has to be changed to 10 pts. Papers must be submitted electronically, in pdf format, by November 29 2018 5:00:00 PM PST using the conference submission site: 
https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/SIGMOD2019

Submissions to the industrial track are not double blind.
