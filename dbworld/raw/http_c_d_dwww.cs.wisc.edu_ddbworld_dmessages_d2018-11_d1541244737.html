International Workshop on Microwave Engineering, Communications Systems and Technologies (MECST?2019)  is an international Workshop that serves researchers, scholars, professionals, students, and academicians who are looking to both foster working relationships and gain access to the latest research results.
We welcome researchers, engineers, students, industry, investors and people from all walks of life interested in Microwave Engineering, Communications Systems and Technologies to attend the conference and share your experiences and lessons with other enthusiasts, and develop opportunities for cooperation.
